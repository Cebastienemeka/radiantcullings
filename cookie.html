Radiant Cullings Cookie Policy

August 2026

Radiant Cullings uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, understand website usage, and provide better services to our visitors.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us improve your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use our website.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies
    These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies
    These help us understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve its performance and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies
    These remember your preferences, such as language or region, to provide a more personalised experience.
  • Marketing Cookies
    Where applicable, these cookies help us deliver relevant communications and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain website features.

Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
